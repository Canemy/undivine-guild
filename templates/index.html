Punctuality

Raids start 20:00 realm time. Invites 15 minutes before that. Raiders should be online when the first invite wave goes out.

Most of our core raiders maintained a 90%+ attendance rate over the course of the entire expansion.

Communication

You're not required to hold speeches every night but being able to communicate and call stuff out when needed is crucial.

Preparing

We expect our members to have the best available consumables and have a good overview of all the boss fights.

We love to analyze our logs and improve our game. Even if you're not in to logs, we can help. You should always work to improve yourself.
