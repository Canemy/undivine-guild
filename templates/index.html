About us

About us

Undivine is a guild formed by people with several Cutting Edge achievements and ex-hardcore experience. Undivine is looking for dedicated players to strengthen its core. We are especially interested in people that are mature and have a considerable raiding experience.

For us, what matters is the player! Although, getting a spot is not easy, and keeping it will be even harder, but if you can dig deep and bring your A game, you will be part of a great team.

Outside of raiding we strive to offer a fun, friendly sometimes mature environment for players to enjoy themselves.

Undivine's goal is to assemble the best team of players for raiding, capable of maximising their characters to push into the top 600 guilds worldwide. Whilst we raid for fun, we also strive to kill current tier bosses as quickly and as efficiently as possible. We want to become one of the premier guilds on Twisting Nether. A guild where it’s members are part of a family, with a healthy positive environment.

We are located on the Twisting Nether (EU)

Things you need to know

Attendance

We raid Wednesday/Sunday/Monday from 19:45 - 23:00. We expect you to show up prior to the raid and stay online throughout its duration. We use players rotation system so that to ensure the best raid composition for the encounter.

Commitment

You must possess a positive attitude towards raiding, especially when it comes to progress raiding. You should be prepared to do everything it takes to maximise your characters effectiveness on the encounter(s).

Communication

Communication during a raid encounter is often important. Whilst you are not required to be speaking every second of an encounter. You should be able to speak and understand English to a reasonable level.

Preparing

Raiders must bring with them best supply of runes, flasks, potions and food available and should have a good overview of all the boss fights.

Theorycrafting

We love to analyze our logs and improve our game. Even if you're not in to logs, we can help. You should always work to improve yourself.

Questions

For further information feel free to add Khan#21626 or hyphyp#2664 on battle.net or reach us in-game.
